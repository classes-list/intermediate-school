🔬 Science Course Overview

The three main areas of science instruction that will be covered are Life Science, Earth Science, and Physical Science. Our lessons include standards-based content with inquiry-based activities. The Scientific Method will be a focus of our learning. Students will be provided with hands-on experiences to explore the lesson concept and will formulate questions that can be answered through investigations.

📚 Curriculum by Quarter

Q1Engineering & Technology + Begin Unit 2 Matter

Identifying problems, improving technology, technology & engineering, improving technology over time, how engineers solve problems, design process. Begin Unit 2 — Matter: measuring mass and volume, evidence of matter, particles.

Q2Finish Unit 2 Matter + Begin Unit 3 Energy in Organisms

Finish Matter: many properties of matter, how heat affects matter, matter can change, when matter changes, conservation of matter. Begin Energy in Organisms: plants use energy & matter, what plants need to grow, how organisms make food, organisms’ growth/change/regrowth, animal energy, energy moves between organisms.

Q3Energy and Matter in Ecosystems

How organisms interact in ecosystems, how the environment affects organisms, living things and their environment, balance in an ecosystem, introduced & invasive species, food webs and food chains, competition in an ecosystem.

Q4Earth Interactions and Resources

Earth materials form systems — interactions of systems shape landforms. Earth’s major systems, natural events on our planet, the water cycle, water stores heat, Earth’s resources and human activities, how to get resources, using resources.

📊 Grading Note for Parents

You will have access to my electronic gradebook via your Beehively account, which will be the primary method of communicating your child’s academic performance. It will be assumed that you have seen all grades and missing assignments that are entered in Beehively.

📝 Homework Policy

Homework that is given will not be for students to explore new concepts. Homework will strictly be to reinforce concepts that have already been taught in class, including research for projects. It is used as more of a “completion” grade. Your children’s teachers will work together to ensure that the students receive a balanced homework regime aimed at creating a positive learning environment that nurtures students’ intellectual growth.

💬 Communication

The best way to communicate with a teacher is by email. Students will be sent home with a white envelope on Tuesdays that has updated work and various other documents regarding events at school.

Updates will also be sent out via Beehively, our electronic communication and grading portal. Announcements posted to Beehively will be sent directly to your email as well as important upcoming assignments and events.
