Religion — Grade 5

Growing in relationship with God through the sacraments, the Apostles’ Creed, the Eucharist, and living as Christians.

📚 Course Units by Quarter

Q1 · Unit 1
💧
The Sacraments
Introduction to the seven sacraments and their meaning in Catholic life.
Topics: Overview of the 7 sacraments; sacred signs of God’s grace; how sacraments shape our lives; sacramental symbols and rituals; Sacraments of Initiation, Healing, and Service (introduction).
Q2 · Unit 2
🕊️
Initiation & The Apostles’ Creed
Sacraments of Initiation (Baptism, Confirmation, Eucharist) and the shared statement of faith.
Topics: Baptism, Confirmation, Eucharist as Initiation sacraments; the Apostles’ Creed line-by-line; what Catholics believe; Advent & Christmas seasons.
🕯️ Liturgical Calendar: Advent
Q3 · Unit 3
🍞
Eucharist & The Last Supper
Deep dive into the Eucharist as the source and summit of Christian life.
Topics: The Last Supper and institution of the Eucharist; parts of the Mass in depth; Real Presence; preparing for and receiving Communion reverently; Lent & Holy Week traditions.
✝️ Liturgical Calendar: Lent
Q4 · Unit 4
🕊️
Forgiving & Living as Christians
The Sacrament of Reconciliation and applying Catholic teaching in daily life.
Topics: Reconciliation (Confession) — steps and importance; forgiveness in family, school, and community; the Beatitudes; corporal & spiritual works of mercy; being a witness of Christ.
