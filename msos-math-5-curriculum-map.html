Curriculum Map by Quarter

enVisionMATH 2.0 Grade 5 · 16 topics grouped into 8 quarterly units. Each row lists Common Core standards, learning objectives, essential content, pacing, assessments, and resources.

Unit Standards Standards Reference Learning Objectives Essential Content & Skills Sequencing & Pacing Assessments Resources Used
Topic 1-2: Place Value & Decimal Add/Subtract CCSS 5.NBT.A.1 (Place value); 5.NBT.A.2 (Powers of 10); 5.NBT.A.3 (Read/write decimals); 5.NBT.A.4 (Round decimals); 5.NBT.B.7 (Add/subtract decimals) 1. Understand place value from thousandths to millions; 2. Read, write, and compare decimals to thousandths; 3. Round decimals; 4. Add and subtract decimals fluently using place-value strategies. Place value chart, powers of 10 (10^0 through 10^6), expanded form, decimal notation, rounding rules, standard algorithm for decimal addition and subtraction. Q1: 4 weeks (8/3-8/28) Topic 1 quiz; Topic 2 quiz; Place-value diagnostic. enVisionMATH 2.0 Vol. 1; Khan Academy 5th grade decimals; PhET: Number Line Distance.
Topic 3-4: Multiplication (Whole Numbers & Decimals) CCSS 5.NBT.B.5 (Fluently multiply multi-digit whole numbers); 5.NBT.B.7 (Multiply decimals) 1. Fluently multiply multi-digit whole numbers using the standard algorithm; 2. Multiply decimals to hundredths using models and place-value reasoning. Standard multiplication algorithm; partial products; area models; multiplying decimals by whole numbers and by decimals; estimating products. Q1: 5 weeks (8/31-10/2) Topic 3 quiz; Topic 4 quiz; Multiplication fluency test; Q1 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; Khan Academy; PhET: Area Model Multiplication.
Topic 5-6: Division (Whole Numbers & Decimals) CCSS 5.NBT.B.6 (Divide multi-digit whole numbers); 5.NBT.B.7 (Divide decimals) 1. Divide multi-digit whole numbers by 2-digit divisors; 2. Divide decimals to hundredths; 3. Interpret quotients and remainders in context. Long division; partial quotients; models for division; dividing decimals by whole numbers; dividing by decimals; interpreting remainders. Q2: 4 weeks (10/19-11/13) Topic 5 quiz; Topic 6 quiz; Long division fluency check. enVisionMATH 2.0; Khan Academy long division videos; PhET: Number Line: Distance.
Topic 7-8: Fraction Add/Subtract & Multiplication CCSS 5.NF.A.1 (Add/subtract fractions with unlike denominators); 5.NF.A.2 (Word problems); 5.NF.B.4 (Multiply fractions); 5.NF.B.5 (Scaling); 5.NF.B.6 (Word problems) 1. Add and subtract fractions with unlike denominators using equivalent fractions; 2. Multiply fractions by fractions and by whole numbers; 3. Solve real-world fraction problems. Common denominators; equivalent fractions; fraction models; multiplying fractions; scaling factor concept; area models for fraction multiplication. Q2: 5 weeks (11/16-12/18) Topic 7 quiz; Topic 8 quiz; Fraction word problems; Q2 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Build a Fraction, Fractions: Equality, Fractions: Mixed Numbers.
Topic 9-10: Fraction Division & Data CCSS 5.NF.B.7 (Divide fractions by whole numbers); 5.MD.B.2 (Line plots with fractions); 5.OA.A.2 (Interpret expressions) 1. Divide unit fractions by whole numbers and whole numbers by unit fractions; 2. Represent and interpret data using line plots and other displays; 3. Solve fraction problems from data. Fraction division models; reciprocals concept (introductory); line plots with fractional measurements; measures of data. Q3: 5 weeks (1/7-2/12) Topic 9 quiz; Topic 10 quiz; Line plot project. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Plinko Probability; DESMOS data activities.
Topic 11-12: Volume & Measurement Conversions CCSS 5.MD.C.3 (Volume concepts); 5.MD.C.4 (Measure volumes); 5.MD.C.5 (V = L×W×H); 5.MD.A.1 (Convert measurement units) 1. Understand volume as an attribute of solid figures; 2. Measure volumes by counting unit cubes; 3. Compute volume using V = L × W × H; 4. Convert between measurement units within the same system. Unit cubes; volume of rectangular prisms; composite figures; metric conversions; customary conversions; multi-step conversion word problems. Q3: 4 weeks (2/15-3/12) Topic 11 quiz; Topic 12 quiz; Volume project; Q3 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Volume; hands-on unit cubes; measurement tools.
Topic 13-14: Numerical Expressions & Coordinate Plane CCSS 5.OA.A.1 (Grouping symbols); 5.OA.A.2 (Write/interpret expressions); 5.G.A.1 (Coordinate plane); 5.G.A.2 (Graph real-world problems) 1. Write and evaluate numerical expressions using order of operations and grouping symbols; 2. Plot points in the first quadrant; 3. Graph real-world problems on the coordinate plane. Order of operations (PEMDAS); grouping symbols (parentheses, brackets, braces); numerical expressions from words; the first quadrant of the coordinate plane; ordered pairs; graphing real-world data. Q4: 4 weeks (3/29-4/23) Topic 13 quiz; Topic 14 quiz; Coordinate graph project. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Graphing Lines (first quadrant use); DESMOS.
Topic 15-16: Patterns & Two-Dimensional Geometry CCSS 5.OA.B.3 (Generate patterns); 5.G.B.3 (Classify shapes by properties); 5.G.B.4 (Classify 2-D figures) 1. Generate and analyze numerical patterns from two related rules; 2. Classify two-dimensional figures by their attributes; 3. Understand hierarchical relationships in shapes. Number patterns; input/output tables; patterns on coordinate plane; hierarchy of quadrilaterals; classification by parallel sides, angles, symmetry. Q4: 5 weeks (4/26-6/3) Topic 15 quiz; Topic 16 quiz; Year-end capstone; Final exam. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Area Builder; NCTM Illuminations.
