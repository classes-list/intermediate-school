enVisionMATH 2.0 Grade 4 · 16 topics grouped into 8 quarterly units. Each row lists Common Core standards, learning objectives, essential content, pacing, assessments, and resources.

Unit Standards Standards Reference Learning Objectives Essential Content & Skills Sequencing & Pacing Assessments Resources Used
Topic 1-2: Place Value & Add/Subtract Multi-Digit CCSS 4.NBT.A.1 (Place value); 4.NBT.A.2 (Read/write, compare); 4.NBT.A.3 (Round); 4.NBT.B.4 (Fluently add/subtract) 1. Generalize place-value understanding for multi-digit whole numbers; 2. Read, write, and compare whole numbers using base-ten numerals, number names, and expanded form; 3. Round to any place; 4. Fluently add and subtract multi-digit whole numbers using the standard algorithm. Place-value chart to millions; base-ten blocks; expanded form; comparing and rounding; standard algorithms for addition and subtraction with regrouping. Q1: 4 weeks (8/3-8/28) Topic 1 quiz; Topic 2 quiz; Place-value diagnostic. enVisionMATH 2.0 Vol. 1; Khan Academy 4th grade place value; PhET: Arithmetic.
Topic 3-4: Multiplication (1-Digit & 2-Digit) CCSS 4.NBT.B.5 (Multi-digit multiplication); 4.OA.A.1-3 (Multiplication concepts & word problems); 4.OA.A.2 (Multiplicative comparison) 1. Use strategies and properties (place-value, distributive, area models) to multiply by 1-digit numbers; 2. Multiply two-digit by two-digit whole numbers; 3. Solve multiplicative-comparison word problems. Area models; partial products; expanded algorithm; standard algorithm; strategies for facts × 10, × 100; comparison problems ('how many times as many'). Q1: 5 weeks (8/31-10/2) Topic 3 quiz; Topic 4 quiz; Multiplication fluency test; Q1 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; Khan Academy multiplication; PhET: Area Model Multiplication.
Topic 5-6: Division & Operations Word Problems CCSS 4.NBT.B.6 (Divide up to 4-digit by 1-digit); 4.OA.A.3 (Multi-step word problems); 4.OA.A.2 (Comparison); 4.OA.A.1 (Equations) 1. Use strategies and properties to divide 4-digit numbers by 1-digit divisors; 2. Interpret remainders in context; 3. Solve multi-step word problems using all four operations. Partial quotients; area models for division; standard long division algorithm; interpreting remainders; multi-step problems with equations; estimation for reasonableness. Q2: 4 weeks (10/19-11/13) Topic 5 quiz; Topic 6 quiz; Division fluency check. enVisionMATH 2.0; Khan Academy long division; base-ten blocks.
Topic 7-8: Factors, Multiples & Fraction Equivalence CCSS 4.OA.B.4 (Factors, multiples, prime/composite); 4.NF.A.1 (Fraction equivalence); 4.NF.A.2 (Compare fractions) 1. Find factor pairs, multiples, prime and composite numbers; 2. Recognize and generate equivalent fractions using visual models and multiplication; 3. Compare fractions with unlike denominators using benchmark and equivalent fractions. Factor pairs; multiples; prime and composite numbers; equivalent fractions with area and set models; benchmark fractions (½); comparing using like denominators or numerators. Q2: 5 weeks (11/16-12/18) Topic 7 quiz; Topic 8 quiz; Fraction diagnostic; Q2 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Build a Fraction, Fractions: Equality.
Topic 9-10: Fraction Add/Sub & Multiply Fractions CCSS 4.NF.B.3 (Add/subtract fractions with like denominators); 4.NF.B.3b (Decompose); 4.NF.B.3c (Mixed numbers); 4.NF.B.4 (Multiply fraction by whole number) 1. Add and subtract fractions with like denominators, including mixed numbers; 2. Decompose fractions in multiple ways; 3. Multiply a fraction by a whole number using models and repeated addition. Adding and subtracting like fractions; renaming mixed numbers; decomposing fractions (3/8 = 1/8 + 2/8); fraction × whole-number multiplication; word problems. Q3: 5 weeks (1/7-2/12) Topic 9 quiz; Topic 10 quiz; Fraction operations project. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Fractions: Mixed Numbers, Build a Fraction.
Topic 11-12: Line Plot Data & Decimals CCSS 4.MD.B.4 (Line plots with fractions); 4.NF.C.6 (Fraction/decimal notation); 4.NF.C.7 (Compare decimals to hundredths); 4.NF.C.5 (Denominators 10 and 100) 1. Represent and interpret data on a line plot using fractional measurements; 2. Understand decimal notation for fractions with denominators 10 and 100; 3. Compare decimals to hundredths. Line plots with 1/2, 1/4, 1/8 measurements; solving problems using data; decimal notation; comparing decimals using place-value understanding; equivalent fractions and decimals. Q3: 4 weeks (2/15-3/12) Topic 11 quiz; Topic 12 quiz; Line-plot data project; Q3 test. enVisionMATH 2.0; PhET: Number Line: Distance; rulers, tape measures.
Topic 13-14: Measurement Equivalence & Patterns CCSS 4.MD.A.1 (Convert measurements); 4.MD.A.2 (Word problems); 4.MD.A.3 (Area & perimeter formulas); 4.OA.C.5 (Generate and analyze patterns) 1. Convert measurement units within the same system (in/ft, cm/m, g/kg, mL/L, min/hr); 2. Solve real-world measurement problems including area and perimeter of rectangles; 3. Generate a number or shape pattern from a given rule and analyze its features. Metric and customary conversions; area and perimeter formulas for rectangles; multi-step measurement problems; input-output tables; shape and number patterns. Q4: 4 weeks (3/29-4/23) Topic 13 quiz; Topic 14 quiz; Measurement lab project. enVisionMATH 2.0; measurement tools (rulers, balances); NCTM Illuminations patterns.
Topic 15-16: Angles, Lines & Two-Dimensional Shapes CCSS 4.MD.C.5-6 (Angle concepts and measurement); 4.MD.C.7 (Angle addition); 4.G.A.1-3 (Points, lines, rays, angles; classify 2-D shapes; symmetry) 1. Recognize angles as a rotation and measure using a protractor; 2. Solve for unknown angles using addition and subtraction; 3. Draw points, lines, line segments, rays, and angles; 4. Classify 2-D figures by their properties and identify lines of symmetry. Angle types (acute, right, obtuse, straight); measuring with a protractor; unknown-angle problems; classifying triangles and quadrilaterals; parallel and perpendicular lines; lines of symmetry. Q4: 5 weeks (4/26-6/3) Topic 15 quiz; Topic 16 quiz; Symmetry art project; Year-end capstone. enVisionMATH 2.0; protractors; PhET: Area Builder; NCTM Illuminations.
