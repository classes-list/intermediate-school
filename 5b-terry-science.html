Science — Grade 5

Building confidence and mastery through inquiry-based Life, Earth, and Physical Science using HMH Into Science.

📚 Course Units by Quarter

Q1 · Unit 1
🏗️
Engineering & Technology + Matter (start)
Identify problems, plan solutions, and start exploring what matter is.
Topics: Identifying problems, improving technology, engineering, design process, how engineers solve problems. Begin Unit 2 — Matter: measuring mass & volume, evidence of matter, particles.
Q2 · Unit 2 → 3
🧪
Finish Matter + Begin Energy in Organisms
How matter changes and how living things get and use energy.
Topics: Properties of matter, how heat affects matter, matter can change, conservation of matter. Begin Unit 3: plants use energy & matter, how organisms make food, growth & regrowth, animal energy, energy moves between organisms.
Q3 · Unit 3
🌿
Energy & Matter in Ecosystems
How organisms interact and the balance of ecosystems.
Topics: How organisms interact in ecosystems, how the environment affects organisms, living things and their environments, balance in an ecosystem, introduced & invasive species, food webs and food chains, competition in an ecosystem.
Q4 · Unit 4
🌎
Earth Interactions & Resources
Earth's systems, how they shape landforms, and human use of natural resources.
Topics: Earth’s major systems, natural events on our planet, the water cycle, water stores heat, Earth’s resources and human activities, how to get resources, using resources wisely.
