About Ms. Soto

Ms. Soto was born in Oregon and moved to Kauaʻi when she was 8 years old. After high school, she moved to Oʻahu to attend Chaminade University, where she earned her degree in Marketing Communications and has continued to live since. She is currently enrolled at Chaminade once again, this time pursuing her degree in Elementary Education. This is her 7th year teaching — and her first year at MSOS.

Ms. Soto has a 16-year-old daughter and is married to her wonderful husband, Ed. She is also mom to five cats! When she’s not in the classroom, she loves shopping, spending time with friends and family, working out, watching movies, and catching up on sleep.
