Religion — Grade 4

Deepening our understanding of Scripture, Catholic teachings, prayer, and moral decision-making while growing in relationship with God.

🌟 Mary, Star of the Sea School Mission

Nurture, Unite, Inspire. We are committed to nurturing each child’s curiosity, uniting our classroom in respect and collaboration, and inspiring every student to grow in knowledge, faith, and wonder for the world around them.

📚 Course Units by Quarter

Q1 · Unit 1
📖
Scripture — God’s Story
Introduction to the Bible, its two testaments, and how Scripture is God’s living Word for the Church today.
Topics: Old & New Testament overview; how to find a Bible passage; God’s covenant with Abraham and Moses; the Gospels; parables of Jesus.
Q2 · Unit 2
💒
The Church & The Sacraments
Learn how the seven sacraments shape our life of faith, with special focus on Baptism, Reconciliation, and Eucharist.
Topics: Seven sacraments; parts of the Mass; roles at Mass; Advent & Christmas seasons; parish life.
🕯️ Liturgical Calendar: Advent
Q3 · Unit 3
🙏
Prayer & Moral Decision-Making
Grow in different kinds of prayer, learn to listen for God, and use the Ten Commandments & Beatitudes to make good choices.
Topics: Types of prayer (adoration, contrition, thanksgiving, supplication); Ten Commandments; Beatitudes; conscience; forgiveness.
✝️ Liturgical Calendar: Lent
Q4 · Unit 4
🌍
Discipleship & Mission
Live as a disciple of Christ by serving others through the works of mercy and being a witness of God’s love in our community.
Topics: Corporal & spiritual works of mercy; Hawaiian saints (Damien, Marianne); vocations; service projects; being Mary’s witnesses.
