English Language Arts — Grade 4

Developing a love for reading, writing, language, and grammar through classic and modern novels, small-group discussion, and daily writing workshop.

📚 Course Units by Quarter

Q1 · Novel 1
✨
Wonder
R.J. Palacio’s modern classic about a boy with a facial difference starting mainstream school. Focus on character, perspective, and kindness.
Focus: Reading comprehension; multiple points of view; personal narrative writing; nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs.
Q2 · Novel 2
🐭
The Tale of Despereaux
Kate DiCamillo’s enchanting fantasy about a tiny mouse with a courageous heart. Deep dive into plot structure and the hero’s journey.
Focus: Plot & theme; creative narrative writing; pronouns, verb tenses, sentence structure.
Q3 · Novel 3
🌾
Inside Out and Back Again
Thanhhà Lại’s novel-in-verse about a young refugee finding her way. Explore free-verse poetry and cross-cultural experience.
Focus: Poetry & figurative language; opinion writing; possessives, commas, quotations; word roots.
Q4 · Novel 4
🕳️
Holes
Louis Sachar’s modern classic weaving parallel plots across generations. Master cause & effect and interlocking narratives.
Focus: Interwoven plots & symbolism; informative writing; comparatives, prefixes/suffixes, dictionary skills.
