Social Studies — Grade 5

The history of North America from the first indigenous tribes through Manifest Destiny, using MyWorld Interactive.

📚 Course Units by Quarter

Q1 · Units 1–3
🌎
Colonization Begins
First Americans → European exploration → settling the colonies.
Topics: Unit 1 — The First Americans: indigenous cultures, tribes, ways of life. Unit 2 — Age of Exploration: European explorers, motivations, encounters. Unit 3 — Settling the Colonies in North America: 13 colonies, geography, and reasons for settlement.
Q2 · Units 4–5
🏛️
Colonial Life & Revolution
Everyday colonial life and the road to American independence.
Topics: Unit 4 — Life in the Colonies: economics, culture, slavery, regional differences. Unit 5 — The American Revolution: causes, key figures, battles, Declaration of Independence, Treaty of Paris.
Q3 · Units 6–7
🦅
A New Nation & The Young Republic
Building a government and life in the early United States.
Topics: Unit 6 — A New Nation: Articles of Confederation, Constitution, Bill of Rights, branches of government. Unit 7 — Life in the Young Republic: early presidents, Louisiana Purchase, War of 1812, growth of the country.
Q4 · Units 8–9
🚂
Westward Expansion + Civil War
Manifest Destiny and the Civil War through Reconstruction.
Topics: Unit 8 — Westward Expansion: Manifest Destiny, Oregon Trail, California Gold Rush, impact on Native Americans. Unit 9 — Civil War and Reconstruction (Parts 1 & 2): causes, key figures, battles, emancipation, rebuilding the nation.
